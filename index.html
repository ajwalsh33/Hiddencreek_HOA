How to Use This System
Annual Planning Workflow:
1Settings — Set your community parameters, dues level, and financial assumptions for the year.
2Annual Budget — Enter your operating budget line by line. Update actuals throughout the year to track spending.
3Capital Plan — List all planned one-time projects with estimated costs and target years.
4Reserve Forecast — Auto-calculated 10-year reserve trajectory. No editing needed — just review and act.
5Scenarios — Test different dues levels and model what happens if homeowners go delinquent on dues.
🏠Homeowner View — A clean, shareable summary of the budget and financials suitable for homeowners. Print or show on screen.
